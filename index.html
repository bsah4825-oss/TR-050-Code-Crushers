Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Data Collection & Usage

We collect and store the following information to provide medication adherence monitoring:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, phone number
  • Medical Data: Medical conditions, allergies, blood type
  • Contact Information: Address, emergency contacts
  • Medication Data: Medication schedules, adherence records
  • Health Metrics: Vital signs (stored locally only)

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • No data is transmitted without your explicit consent
  • You can delete all your data at any time

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your stored data
  • Correction: Update your information
  • Deletion: Remove all your data permanently
  • Portability: Export your data in readable format
